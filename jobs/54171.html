Job Description:

Wanted: coding ninjas who are fast learners, great communicators, and ping pong or foosball gurus wanting to join one of the Top Employers for Young People and top 5 SaaS companies in Canada!

Vision Critical provides technology and expertise for our clients to build and manage online insights communities, engaging with their own consumers and stakeholders on a regular basis through the use of online surveys, discussions, videos and 3D simulations. Our client base includes one third of the world’s top 100 brands and our top-notch professionals are spread across the globe in North America, Europe, Australia, Asia and South Africa.

Software Developer (8 month contract) – May 2015

Who You Are:

A Computer Science or Engineering student wanting to prove yourself, test your limit, build great software and be mentored by top notch developers
Team player who will contribute to an agile team as an engineer building our industry leading SaaS product suite
Driven, focused and results-oriented always looking to learn cutting-edge technologies
You want to open doors to possibly start your career with one of the Top SaaS companies in Canada
Very confident with at least one modern programming language
Enjoy learning from hands on experiences such as Hackathons and lunch and learns
You embrace a work/life balance so you can enjoy all that Vancouver has to offer

Ready to join our team?
